## 📌 Overview  
**Recruva** is an AI-powered recruitment platform that simplifies hiring by automating job management, candidate tracking, and interview workflows. It uses intelligent algorithms to match candidates with job requirements, helping organizations hire faster and smarter.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement  
Traditional hiring systems are:  
- Slow and manual  
- Hard to track applicants  
- Inefficient in evaluation  
- Prone to bias  

**Recruva** solves these challenges with automation, AI insights, and a centralized recruitment system.

---

## 💡 Solution  
Recruva provides:  
- Centralized Applicant Tracking System (ATS)  
- AI-powered candidate evaluation  
- Automated workflows  
- Real-time interview management  

---

## ✨ Features  

### 🧾 Job Management  
- Create and approve job postings  
- Define skills, experience, and salary  

### 📢 Job Posting  
- Publish jobs and track applications  

### 👩‍💼 Candidate Management  
- Resume parsing  
- Candidate filtering and ranking  
- Structured candidate profiles  

### 📅 Interview Scheduling  
- Schedule interviews with meeting links  
- Track interviewer & scheduler info  
- Real-time status updates  

### 📊 Dashboard  
- Hiring insights and analytics  
- Candidate pipeline tracking  

---

## 🤖 AI & Automation  

Recruva integrates **Generative AI and Semantic Matching**:

- Resume Parsing – Extract structured candidate data  
- Smart Matching – Compare job requirements with candidate skills  
- AI Summarization – Generate candidate summaries  
- Multi-language Support – Translate candidate data  
- Powered by GROQ API and LLMs
